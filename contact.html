Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a career guidance session?
You can schedule a career guidance session by filling out the contact form above or calling our support number. Our career counselors will reach out within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.
What services do you offer for freshers?
We offer comprehensive services including resume building, interview preparation, skill development workshops, and placement assistance specifically designed for freshers and recent graduates.
Do you provide placement guarantee?
While we don't guarantee placements, we have a 95% success rate in helping candidates secure positions through our extensive network of 500+ partner companies and personalized career support.
Can companies partner with ElevateJobs?
Absolutely! We welcome corporate partnerships. Please reach out to our partnership team at partnerships@elevatejobs.com or select "Partnership Opportunities" in the contact form.
What is the response time for queries?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support hotline for immediate assistance.
Do you offer online consultation?
Yes, we provide both in-person and online consultation services via video calls. Our services are available nationwide with flexible scheduling options.
